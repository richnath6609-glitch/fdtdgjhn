The Arima Legacy

Supporting Artisans, Preserving Heritage

Established in 2023, Arima 111 Collections was born out of a desire to bridge the gap between traditional West African craftsmanship and the modern global aesthetic.

Every thread woven into our Kente and every dye used in our Batik represents the livelihood of local Ghanaian artisans. We are committed to ethical production and the preservation of weaving techniques passed down through generations.
